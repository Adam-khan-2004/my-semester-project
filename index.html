Welcome to Allai Valley

Allai Valley is a mountainous region in Hazara Division, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, known for its meadows, forests, streams, and cultural heritage.

Scenic Glimpse of Allai

History of Allai Valley

1595 – 1971

Allai was an independent tribal state ruled by the Nawabs of Allai from the Bebal subsection of the Gabri Swati tribe.

Resistance Era

Nawab Arsala Khan Swati resisted British rule. Pokal was the historical capital.

Ethnic Diversity

Swatis and Gujjars inhabit the valley. Gujjars traditionally speak Hindko and also Pashto.

2005 Earthquake

The earthquake transformed traditional mud houses into modern concrete structures.

District Status

Allai became a district on January 23, 2023. Current MNA: Prince Muhammad Nawaz.

Culture & Lifestyle

Men wear Shalwar Kameez with Pakol. Women wear Burqa or Padhune.
